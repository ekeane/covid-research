Google Trends Research

Breakdown of popular keywords trending in BC. Click rising on the Google trend modules to see up to date insights.

Search Term: Part-time job

As people sadly lose jobs people have also been looking for part-time courses as many likely think this is a great time to start a career change as they want a more stable role for the future. A common search trend has been masters courses and BCIT part time courses.

Search Term: Remote and VPN

Remote could mean many things to working remotely or using a remote on a TV. From what I observed many people need new technologies to enable them to work from home such as Mircosoft remote desktop, remote connection managers, or VPNs.

Search Term: Meeting

It seems as though many people people in BC are trying to figure out how they can communicate within their businesses and to existing clients. They're looking into how they can run their businesses and keep events such as townhalls and web conferences online.

Search Term: Marketing

Right now there seems to be a large demand for Master's degrees as many people recently graduated or who will be graduating soon feel like they will not land a job in this current market. It seems as though many are contacting different marketing agencies like Vector Marketing to deal with the COVID-19 situation.

Search Term: permit

There has been a surge in BC for study permit extensions as many people now need to extend degrees due to this pandemic. People have also been looking significantly at technical safety BC.

Search Term: Housing

As the pandemic grows BC residents are looking for other housing options and short term rentals. There is a significant surge in this area.
